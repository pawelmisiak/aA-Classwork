Peanut Butter and Jelly Heaven

    Description Ingredients Directions

Description

The peanut butte r and jelly sandwich that will fill you to the brim with such goodness you will think you are in heaven. The jelly must be hand made and the peanut butter must be organic, and it must be on whole wheat bread because we are healthy little hipsters.

Peanut Butter and Jelly Wiki

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter (ORGANIC)
  • Jelly (HANDMADE)
  • WHOLE WHEAT bread
  • some CrEaTiViTy

Directions

  1. You take the best two pieces of bread you can find, from the dead center of the bread.
  2. Toast that bread like your life depends on it. EXTRA dark. BLACK. CRISPLY.
  3. Peanut Butter first. This is crucial. Spread that peanut butter.
  4. Spread the jelly like your life depends on it. Make sure you get a nice even coat.
  5. NOW ARE YOU READY???? Combine the two slices. ENJOY LIKE YOUR LIFE DEPENDS ON IT.
